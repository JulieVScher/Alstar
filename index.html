About us

ABA in the GTA Autism Behaviour Therapy is an approved Ontario Autism Program (OAP) provider specializing in Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA) Therapy throughout the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).

Owned and operated by a Board Certified Behaviour Analyst® (BCBA®).
